## üìù Job Description

```
At Boeing, we innovate and collaborate to make the world a better place.

Boeing Global Services (BGS) has an exciting opportunity for an Associate or Experienced Finance Analyst. This role will provide financial support to Boeing Converted Freighters and Maintenance Services within the BGS Cabin, Modifications, Maintenance, and Digital Services portfolio.

This position allows telecommuting. The selected candidate will be required to work onsite 3 to 4 days a week.

Organizations Supported:
- Boeing Converted Freighters: Converts passenger airplanes into cargo airplanes (737, 767 models) and collects royalties on all Boeing models converted by 3rd parties
- Maintenance Services: Portfolio of MRO services, Customer Support, Airplane On-Ground teams, and joint ventures

Position Responsibilities:
- Support with timely and reliable financial information
- Be effective in every aspect of the business as a value integrator
- Create and maintain resource forecasts (employment, non-labor dollars, overhead, offloads, work placement)
- Extract data from multiple sources and compile routine performance reports
- Assist with preparing variance analyses
- Maintain chargeline structure, communicate charging instructions, initiate CLAS offloads and WPRs
- Create and maintain resource plans at Senior Manager level for Long Range Business Plan
- Create, maintain, and investigate variances to quarterly Non-Recurring Program forecasts
- Support the monthly Business Plan Review
- Proactively drive cost investigation and reduction
- Perform ad hoc analysis and reporting
- Identify opportunities for continuous process improvement
- Assist with defining requirements and deployment of common systems, tools, processes
```
