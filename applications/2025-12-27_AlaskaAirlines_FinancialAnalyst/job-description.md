Financial Analyst II: Responsible for developing solutions to treasury and finance processes. Key Duties (FA I):
- Administers portfolio of aircraft debt, operating leases, and credit facilities
- Maintains accurate records and forecasts of financial obligations
- Processes payments and validates wire activity per fraud control processes
- Monitors airline industry credit conditions and rating agency assessments
- Maintains financial reporting and audit documentation for SOX compliance
- Works with team to forecast, analyze cash flow obligations, balance sheet metrics
- Prepares monthly reports for accounting and serves as liaison with audit teams
- Provides input into continuous enhancement of reports and processes

Key Duties (FA II):
- Takes initiative to develop solutions for debt, lease, and credit facility portfolio
- Administers finance concepts to make recommendations on financing/fleet transactions
- Trains team in monitoring airline industry credit conditions
- Works independently to forecast, analyze, consolidate balance sheet information
- Creates and influences cross-functionally on monthly reports
- Makes recommendations to improve internal controls, efficiency, and reduce costs
