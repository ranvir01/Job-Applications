## üìù Job Description

```
Job Mission:
Terraformation's mission is to accelerate global forest restoration as a critical climate solution. The Project Finance (PF) team plays a central role by securing and structuring the capital needed to launch and scale restoration projects.

The Project Finance Execution Manager will directly advance this mission by driving project and acquisition financings from term sheet through close, ensuring rigorous due diligence, effective risk allocation, and seamless coordination across internal experts and external partners.

Responsibilities:
- Coordinate internal and external stakeholders as you lead the execution of project financing transactions from term sheet through closing, including due diligence and document negotiation
- Manage cross-functional collaboration with project developers, ecology and carbon teams, legal, tax, and other technical consultants
- Review and negotiate project finance transaction documents and project contracts to effectively assign risk
- Interact with and interpret the financial model to quantify project risks, and support contract negotiation positions
- Coordinate closing processes, manage deadlines, and adapt to evolving deal parameters
- Build strong working relationships with counterparties, project partners, and potential investors
- Collaborate with team members across time zones
```
