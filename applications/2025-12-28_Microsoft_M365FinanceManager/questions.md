**Action:** I built an EBITDA-based model with revenue as the primary driver. Created sensitivity tables showing how variations in growth rate, margin, and multiple affected valuation. **Result:** Delivered a $1.45M-$1.75M valuation range. The driver-based structure helped stakeholders understand not just the number, but what levers mattered most. They used it for strategic planning discussions with potential buyers.
