## üìù Job Description

Microsoft's Finance Rotation Program (FRP) is one of the premier financial analysis programs in tech. This opportunity is an investment in early-in-profession talent, offering training within core finance teams that are driving industry-leading work. Analysts take on some of the biggest challenges in Finance and have the chance to work abroad, all while building a strong community with their cohort.

**Program Structure:**
- Two-year rotational program
- Up to four, six-month rotations in Financial Planning & Analysis roles
- Submit preferences for teams across corporate finance, sales, and product groups like Azure, M365, and Gaming
- Use best-in-class AI tools to solve complex business problems
- Develop finance skills rapidly, contribute meaningfully, and interact with senior leaders
- Gain insights and mentorship to accelerate your career

**Microsoft's Mission:** Empower every person and every organization on the planet to achieve more.

**Culture:** Growth mindset, innovate to empower others, and collaborate to realize shared goals. Values of respect, integrity, and accountability to create a culture of inclusion.
