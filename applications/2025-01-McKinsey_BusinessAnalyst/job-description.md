## Company Overview
McKinsey & Company is a global management consulting firm that helps organizations across industries solve problems, create value, and accelerate sustainable and inclusive growth. Founded in 1926, McKinsey has 65+ offices in over 100 countries and serves clients from Fortune 500 companies to innovative startups.

## Role Overview
As a Business Analyst at McKinsey, you join client service teams to solve complex challenges through research, data analysis, stakeholder interviews, and collaborative problem-solving. The role requires ownership of workstreams, delivering actionable solutions, and driving long-term change for influential organizations.

## Key Responsibilities
- **Client-Facing Work:** Collaborate directly with clients to understand challenges, refine strategies, and implement transformations
- **Problem-Solving:** Combine research, data analysis, interviews, and brainstorming to generate fresh insights and breakthrough solutions
- **Workstream Ownership:** Take ownership of project components, managing tasks from initiation through completion
- **Deliverable Creation:** Produce high-quality work products including PowerPoint presentations, Excel models, articles, and written deliverables
- **Cross-Functional Collaboration:** Work alongside McKinsey colleagues and client teams to drive results
- **Impact Creation:** Ensure recommendations are effective, long-lasting, and equip clients with tools for continued improvement

## Requirements

- **Time Management:** Exceptional time management in complex, autonomous work environments
