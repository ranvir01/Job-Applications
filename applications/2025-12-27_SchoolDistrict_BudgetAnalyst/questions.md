## üè¢ Research Notes

### About School District Finance
- Public school districts receive funding from federal, state, and local sources
- Grants have specific compliance requirements and reporting deadlines
- OSPI (Office of Superintendent of Public Instruction) is Washington State's education agency
- Chart of accounts provides standardized coding for revenue and expenditure tracking

### Grant Management Context
- Federal grants (Title I, Title II, IDEA, etc.) have strict compliance rules
- "Time and effort" documentation tracks how grant-funded employees spend their time
- Grant budgets must be reconciled and reported to grantors
- Non-compliance can result in findings and fund recovery

### Key Terms
- **OSPI**: Washington State Office of Superintendent of Public Instruction
- **Chart of Accounts**: Standardized coding structure for budgets
- **Time and Effort**: Documentation for grant-funded positions
- **Budget Revision**: Formal process to modify adopted budget
