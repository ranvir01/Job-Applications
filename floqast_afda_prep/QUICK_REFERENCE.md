> "Two-column approach: Start with GL cash balance on one side, bank statement balance on the other. Adjust GL for bank charges and interest not recorded. ### Variance Analysis
> "Compare actuals to budget or prior month. Identify material variancesâ€”greater than 10% or $5,000. Break down drivers: Is it volume, price, or timing? > "Day 1-3: Accrue expenses for services received but not invoiced. Day 4-5: Bank reconciliations. Day 6: Depreciation. Day 7: Variance analysis. Day 8-10: Financial statement review. FloQast's Close product manages this workflow, tracking status and dependencies."
