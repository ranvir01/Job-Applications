### Opening Paragraph (Hook + Position)

*Grab attention and state the role you're applying for.*

> Dear [Hiring Manager/Recruiting Team],

> I am excited to apply for the [Position Title] role at [Company Name]. [Hook: Why you're interested / connection to company / compelling opening that shows you've done research].

---

### Body Paragraph 1 (Relevant Experience)

*Highlight your most relevant experience with specific achievements.*

> In my role as [Position] at [Company], I [specific achievement with numbers]. This experience has prepared me to [how it connects to the job requirements]. For example, [brief STAR story if space allows].

---

### Body Paragraph 2 (Skills & Fit)

*Connect additional skills and explain why you're a great fit.*

> Beyond my [type of experience], I bring [additional relevant skills]. At [University/Company], I [another achievement or skill demonstration]. I am particularly drawn to [Company Name] because [specific reason tied to company values/mission/culture].

---

### Closing Paragraph (Call to Action)

*Express enthusiasm and request an interview.*

> I am eager to bring my [key skills] to [Company Name] and contribute to [specific team/initiative]. I would welcome the opportunity to discuss how my background aligns with your needs. Thank you for considering my application.

> Sincerely,
> [Your Name]

## ðŸ’¡ Tips for Customization

### Research Hooks
- Recent company news or announcements
- Company values that resonate with you
- Specific products/services you admire
- Connections (informational interviews, events)

### Achievement Formula
`[Action Verb] + [Task] + [Result with Numbers]`

Example: "Developed a financial model that identified $50K in cost savings"

### Company-Specific Angles
| Company Type | Angle to Emphasize |
|--------------|-------------------|
| **Startup** | Adaptability, wearing multiple hats, passion for the mission |
| **Big Tech** | Scalability, data-driven approach, innovation |
| **Finance** | Attention to detail, analytical rigor, precision |
| **Consulting** | Client focus, problem-solving, communication |
